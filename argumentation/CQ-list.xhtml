Critical Questions (information list)

  • How confident are you that this assumption is correct?
  • What explains your degree of confidence?
  • What circumstances or information might undermine this assumption?
  • Could this assumption have been true in the past, but less so now?
  • Are there lots of calls to share the article or join mailing lists?
  • How aggressively are adverts and calls to share placed?
  • Does the title of the article reflect the content? Does it have a "clickbait" title?
  • How engaged is the author with the platform the content appears on?
  • Is content aggressive and/or very bold?
  • How objective and unbiased is the language?
  • Is the tone emotive or exaggerated?
  • Is language used that qualifies the degree of confidence in the claim?
  • Is the claim justified with convincing evidence?
  • Can the content be independently fact-checked?
  • Is supporting evidence accurately represented?
  • What is the evidence for the charge of bias?
  • Is this a type of dialogue that requires a participant to take evidence on both sides of an issue into account?
