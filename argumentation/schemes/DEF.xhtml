Definition

This scheme warrants a CLAIM as the definition of a class or concept. DATA identifies the concept defined. Any uncertainty about the definition, or information that qualifies the context in which it applies, may be expressed as a QUALIFIER.

Its purpose is to bring to bear critical questions about the definition:

  • If the claim defines a class, what are the defining properties of that class?

  • Is there a test that something meeting this definition?

  • Can you give an example?
